A creative app designed to spark children's imaginations by guiding them through the story-making process.

## Overview

The Kids Story Making App is an interactive tool that empowers children to craft their own stories. With a simple, intuitive interface, kids can choose characters, pick settings, and build narratives step by step.

## Features

- **Character Creator** — Kids can design their own characters with names, traits, and appearances
- **Story Templates** — Pre-built story structures to help get the creative juices flowing
- **Interactive Prompts** — Fun prompts and plot twists to keep the story moving forward
- **Illustration Support** — Simple illustrations that bring stories to life
- **Save & Share** — Finished stories can be saved and shared with family and friends
